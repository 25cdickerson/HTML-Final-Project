Bio

Drew Hunter (born September 5, 1997) is an American middle distance runner from Purcellville, Virginia. He set the national indoor mile record for high school boys in 2016. He was named Gatorade National Cross-Country Runner of the Year in 2016. He committed to the University of Oregon on November 12, 2015. However he signed a 10-year contract with Adidas on July 8, 2016 and pursued running professionally instead.
